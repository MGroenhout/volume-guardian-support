Frequently Asked Questions

How does Volume Guardian work?

Volume Guardian protects your child's hearing by automatically limiting and monitoring volume levels.

Can I adjust the volume limit?

Yes, you can adjust the volume limit through the app settings. Drag the slider to set your desired limit.

Does the app work in the background?

Yes, Volume Guardian stays active in the background for continuous protection. Keep the app open for optimal performance.

Privacy Policy

All your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Your privacy is fully protected as no data leaves your device. Volume Guardian operates completely offline.
